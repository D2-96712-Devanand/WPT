The two primary sections of an HTML document are :
  1. HEAD
    • The Title tag in HEAD sections gives the title to the Page.
  2. BODY
    • Whatever is written in the BODY tag appears on the brpwser window.
    • Unordered lists can be created using the ul tag followed by li that list out the bulleted
      text. Li tag can have the attribute, type = circle or disc or square
    • Ordered lists are created using the ol tag followed by li tags that can have the attribute type =
      1       or a or A or I

strike through or it could also be subcripted or superscripted depending upon the need of creator.

to various combinations of RCB values.
